Summer Cheng, Clinical Medicine, Fudan University

Pinellia ternata (Chinese: 半夏) is known as the herb effective in removing dampness-phlegm, an important and common pathological factors in traditional Chinese medicine. I fell in love with this kind of plant at first sight.

As a girl with herb personality of Pinellia ternata, my ambition is to relieve the pain and provide comfort.
